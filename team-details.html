Message From The Chairman

Dr. Laxmi Prasad Devkota

It is with great pride and responsibility that I extend this message on behalf of the Board of Directors of Sabhapokhari Hydropower Limited. As Chairman, I am honored to be part of an organization that stands at the forefront of Nepal's transition toward clean and self-sustaining energy development.

Nepal is blessed with immense hydropower potential, and our company was founded with a deep commitment to unlocking this energy in ways that are both economically viable and environmentally sound. The Lankhuwa Khola Small Hydroelectric Project, with its 5 MW capacity, is a strategic step toward that vision. It is not just a project — it is a symbol of progress for rural development, energy independence, and national prosperity.

Our board remains dedicated to upholding high standards of corporate governance, financial transparency, and risk management. We are equally committed to creating long-term value for our investors, while also fulfilling our social and environmental responsibilities. We see every challenge as an opportunity to innovate and every milestone as a foundation for future growth.

I would like to express my gratitude to our investors, employees, government authorities, and local communities who have all played a vital role in the journey so far. Your belief in our mission motivates us to aim higher and deliver more.

As we look ahead, our focus remains clear — to build resilient infrastructure, to lead with integrity, and to ensure that Sabhapokhari Hydropower Limited continues to contribute meaningfully to Nepal’s energy security and economic advancement.

Thank you for your continued trust.

Sincerely,

Dr. Laxmi Prasad Devkota
Chairman
Sabhapokhari Hydropower Limited
Banasthali, Kathmandu

Message From The Managing Director

Er. Shree Ram Devkota

Welcome to Sabhapokhari Hydropower Limited! It gives me immense pleasure to share the journey and vision of SPHPL as we move forward with our mission to contribute to Nepal’s sustainable energy future. Our flagship undertaking, the Lankhuwa Khola Small Hydroelectric Project (5 MW), located in Sabhapokhari Rural Municipality, Sankhuwasabha, reflects our commitment to harnessing clean and renewable hydropower efficiently and responsibly.

Despite challenges in remote terrains, our dedicated team, partners, and local stakeholders have shown unwavering determination and collaboration. The twin headworks and meticulously engineered headrace system are key components in our drive toward quality, reliability, and long-term operational success.

Sabhapokhari Hydropower Limited is not just a power producer — we are a catalyst for local development, economic progress, and environmental stewardship. We believe in transparency, innovation, and inclusive growth to ensure our efforts benefit communities while meeting Nepal’s growing energy needs.

As we move toward commissioning, I sincerely thank all investors, government bodies, financial partners, and the local community for their trust and support.

Together, let us light up the future — sustainably.

Er. Shree Ram Devkota
Managing Director
Sabhapokhari Hydropower Limited
Banasthali, Kathmandu
